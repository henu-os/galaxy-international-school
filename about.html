Galaxy International School

“Where Learning Meets Excellence” — Established in 2009 to bring quality English medium RBSE education to Auwa, Pali.

Our Story

A Mission of Quality Education

Galaxy International School (GIS) is an English medium, co-educational RBSE school (Nursery to Class XII Science) focused on strong academics, English communication, values, discipline, and holistic development.

  • Established: 2009
  • RBSE Board • Nursery to XII (Science)
  • JEE–NEET Foundation Coaching (VIII–XII)
  • Smart classrooms, labs, library, sports, hostel
